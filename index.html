MEMPHIS WEATHER

Hey! You're probably here because you found this website on my LinkedIn page. Feel free to look around and check out some projects I've worked on, my social media pages, or even peruse my résumé.

Born and raised in the 'Show-Me' state, I'm a proud member of the University of Missouri graduating class of '09, M-I-Z! Shortly after graduation I moved to Memphis for what I thought would be 10 weeks to work for the non-profit Service Over Self (SOS). Ten years later I'm still here and love being a Memphian. In that time I've met and married my wife, adopted two pups, bought a home, made lifelong friends, and watched as this city has changed before my eyes. For the past six years I've been a valuable member of the grounds staff at AutoZone Park where I've served as Director of Field Operations since 2018. Aditionally, you'll see in the 'projects' section below, that I've recently decided to pursue my coding interests by taking the Web Development class through Tech901.
